2 Units Semi Detached Residences

Experience unparalleled luxury with our Four-family residence, meticulously designed in a sophisticated contemporary style. Spread across two units and two levels, each semi-detached residence boasts five spacious bedrooms, offering both privacy and comfort. Nestled on a generous 1350sqm site, the property features ample parking and beautifully cultivated landscaping, perfect for outdoor activities and serene living.

Proposed Prototype Residences – Cowrie Creek Estate

Introducing the Cowrie Creek Estate, a pinnacle of luxury living situated in the heart of the Lekki Peninsula. Developed for the NLNG Staff Cooperative Investment & Credit Society (NLNG Coop), this estate offers three distinctive prototype designs: Basic, Classic, and Luxury. Each design embodies strong modernist architectural elements with exquisite aesthetic appeals, perfectly harmonizing with the surrounding environment. Tailored to meet the diverse desires of its members, Cowrie Creek Estate sets a new standard for upscale residential communities.

Lekki Luxury Flats - Interior Re-Modelling

Transforming living spaces into luxurious, inviting homes, our interior re-modelling project for Lekki Luxury Flats fosters a vibrant and socially connected lifestyle while preserving the essential need for privacy and individuality. By integrating cutting-edge design features and modern aesthetics, we've turned this space into a sophisticated urban sanctuary, perfect for contemporary living.

Studio Apartments

Designed for the modern urban dweller, our Studio Apartments offer a compact yet dynamic living environment. Featuring airy elegance and the innovative use of clear glass doors, these spaces introduce flexibility and modern flair. Elevated bed spaces above active living areas, complemented by trendy furniture pieces, create charming focal points that maximize functionality within restrained spaces.

Redevelopment of Ultra-Modern Market

In partnership with local stakeholders, we spearheaded the redevelopment of an existing spare parts dealers market into a state-of-the-art facility. This Public-Private Partnership initiative involved complete demolition of the old structures and the creation of a modern market infrastructure. Our design prioritized cost-effectiveness, adherence to space standards, and the selection of durable materials suitable for the local climate, ensuring a functional and enduring market space that meets the client's vision.

Proposed Emzor Pharmaceutical Warehouse

Our design for the Emzor Pharmaceutical Warehouse emphasizes functionality and efficiency across its multi-level spaces. This utilitarian facility is crafted to provide a safe and comfortable working environment, enhancing productivity and reducing operational costs. With a focus on corporate image and worker satisfaction, the design integrates robust building aesthetics, thoughtful landscaping, and comprehensive safety features to support the client's operational goals.

Luxury Residence, Mayfair Garden

Designed with elegance and simplicity, the Mayfair Garden Luxury Residence is a testament to refined living. This modern four-bedroom house features a well-planned layout, exquisite interior and exterior decoration, and abundant natural lighting through numerous windows. The harmonious blend of sleek design elements and functional spaces creates an inviting atmosphere that captivates every observer, showcasing our dedication to superior architectural craftsmanship.

Worship Center, Livingspring Parish, RCCG

Transforming an abandoned swimming pool into a vibrant place of worship, our design for the Livingspring Parish RCCG Worship Center accommodates 400 worshipers. The facility includes specialized halls for youths, teenagers, children, a media room, and church offices. Our design concept emphasizes digital integration, multi-use spaces, and efficient layouts, creating an inviting and functional environment that serves both spiritual and community needs.

Proposed Hotel Design, Ikota, Lekki, Lagos

Designed to elevate the skyline of Ikota, Lekki, our proposed hotel is set to become an iconic landmark along the Lekki axis. The contemporary design seamlessly integrates cozy gardens with lush, undulating structures, creating a visually stunning and luxurious experience. Our approach breaks traditional boundaries, blending cultural influences with modern aesthetics to offer guests an exceptional ambiance that fosters relaxation and indulgence.

HUBMART SUPERMARKET, Adeola Odeku, Victoria Island

Reimagining the traditional shopping experience, our design for HUBMART SUPERMARKET in Adeola Odeku, Victoria Island, focuses on flexibility and ambiance. By challenging conventional shopping models, we created a layout that enhances customer interaction, awareness, simplification, and pleasure. The new format ensures an easy and enjoyable shopping journey, reflecting our dedication to translating customer values into a tailored and exceptional retail environment.

Diamond Bank

As part of Diamond Bank's strategic expansion, our team was entrusted with designing modern and exclusive banking environments across various branches nationwide. Each design focuses on creating a welcoming atmosphere for both staff and customers, emphasizing exclusivity and exceptional banking experiences. Our innovative layouts and refined aesthetics reflect the bank's commitment to professionalism and customer satisfaction.

Heritage Bank

In collaboration with Heritage Bank, we crafted luxurious and welcoming branch environments that enhance the customer experience. Each branch features a deluxe welcome center designed to serve customers exclusively, creating a lush and elegant ambiance. The dynamic modern architecture, including clear glass walls, introduces flexibility and sophistication into the office interiors, reflecting Heritage Bank's dedication to providing outstanding service in a refined setting.

Proposed Province Headquarters – Redeemed Christian Church of God

Set against a breathtaking ocean view, our design for the Province Headquarters of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) in Osborne Phase 2, Ikoyi, Lagos, is a beacon of contemporary architecture and spiritual harmony. This expansive facility accommodates 1,000 worshipers and includes specialized halls for youths, teenagers, children, a media room, and church offices. Designed for versatility, the headquarters also supports commercial functions such as events and music production. Our architectural concept integrates digital advancements, multi-use spaces, and efficient layouts, ensuring the building is both aesthetically pleasing and functionally robust, making it an attractive landmark in its environs.
